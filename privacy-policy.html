1. Introduction

Welcome to SERPSnap ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website at tools.serpsnap.abrdns.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our Site.

By using SERPSnap, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Site.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SERPSnap is designed with privacy as a priority. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information unless you contact us voluntarily.
  • All SEO tools on SERPSnap run entirely in your browser. Any content you type or paste (titles, descriptions, articles) is never sent to our servers.
  • We do not store or share your content with any third party.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-personal technical information when you visit the Site, including:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic location (country/city level only)

This information is collected through Google Analytics and is used solely to understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it. We do not use this data to identify individual users.

4. Cookies

SERPSnap uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand how visitors interact with the Site.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to display relevant advertisements to visitors. These cookies may track your browsing activity across websites.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences within the Site (such as your last selected tool tab).

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on the Site.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our Site, please visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other websites they visited prior to visiting our Site.

We use the information we get from Google Analytics only to improve the Site. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit the Site, not your name or other identifying information.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

SERPSnap is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information promptly.

9. Data Security
