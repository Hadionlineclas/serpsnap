About SERPSnap

We built free SEO tools that anyone can use — no signup, no credit card, no confusion.

Our Story

SERPSnap started with a simple frustration. Every time we needed to check how a page title would look on Google, or verify the keyword density of a blog post, we had to either pay for expensive tools or sign up for yet another platform.

So we built our own. A clean, fast, free suite of SEO tools that just work — no account needed, no paywalls, no tricks. Just open the page and start using it.

Today, SERPSnap helps thousands of bloggers, freelancers, digital marketers, and small business owners every month. And it's still completely free.

Our Tools

Right now, SERPSnap offers six free SEO tools:

SERP Preview Tool — See exactly how your page will appear in Google search results, on both desktop and mobile. Check your title length, description quality, and get a live SEO score.

Meta Tag Generator — Generate complete HTML meta tags including Open Graph and Twitter Card tags in one click. Just fill in your page details and copy the code.

Keyword Density Checker — Paste your content and instantly see which keywords appear most often and at what density. Avoid keyword stuffing and find gaps in your content.

Word Counter & Readability Score — Count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs. Get a readability score so you know if your content is easy enough for your audience to read.

URL Slug Generator — Turn any title into a clean, SEO-friendly URL slug. Remove stop words, control formatting, and generate multiple variations.

OG Tag Preview — See how your page looks when shared on Facebook and WhatsApp. Generate the correct Open Graph and Twitter Card HTML tags.

✅ All tools are 100% free, work in your browser, and require zero signup. We plan to keep adding more tools over time based on what our users need.
