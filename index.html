          What is a URL slug?

          It's the last part of your URL: yoursite.com/this-part-here

          Short, keyword-rich slugs rank better on Google.
        

      

      

        
Generated Slugs

        
Full URL:

Slug only:

        
your-slug-here

        

        

          SEO slug tips:

          ✔ Keep it under 5 words · ✔ Include your main keyword · ✔ Use hyphens, never underscores · ✔ Avoid numbers unless essential
